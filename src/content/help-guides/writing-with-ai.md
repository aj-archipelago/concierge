## Writing with AI Assistance

The Write feature helps you draft, edit, and refine content with AI support.

### Getting Started

1. Go to **Write** from the sidebar
2. You'll see a rich text editor with an integrated AI chat panel
3. Start typing your content or ask the AI to help you draft something

### AI-Assisted Writing

- Use the **chat panel** on the right to ask for help
- Request drafts, outlines, rewrites, or edits
- The AI can help with headlines, summaries, and formatting

### Editor Features

- **Rich text formatting** — bold, italic, headings, lists, and more
- **Save** — use the save button to store your article to your file storage

### Tips

- Be specific in your prompts for better results
- Use the chat to iterate on drafts — ask for revisions or alternatives
- You can copy content between the editor and chat freely
