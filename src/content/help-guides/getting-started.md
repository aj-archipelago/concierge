## Getting Started with Concierge

Concierge is your AI-powered assistant for content creation, translation, transcription, and more. Here's how to get up and running quickly.

### Your Home Page

When you log in, you'll land on the **Home** page. This is your dashboard showing a digest of recent activity and quick access to all features.

### Navigation

Use the **sidebar** on the left to navigate between features:

- **Chat** — Have conversations with the AI assistant
- **Translate** — Translate content between languages
- **Transcribe** — Convert audio and video to text
- **Write** — Draft and edit content with AI assistance
- **Applets** — Use and create custom AI-powered mini-apps
- **Media** — Manage your uploaded files

### Quick Tips

- Click your **profile avatar** in the top-right → **Settings** to personalize Concierge — choose your AI model, language, theme, manage memory, connectors, and skills
- Type **`/`** in any chat input to see available commands
- Use the **bell icon** to check on background tasks like translations and transcriptions
- The **chat icon** in the header opens a docked chat panel alongside any page
- Use **Send feedback** in the sidebar to report an issue, share an idea, or ask a question. You can include a screenshot; Concierge also sends the current page context so the team can investigate.
- Concierge can also submit feedback from chat when the assistant notices a confusing or broken workflow. If the assistant already has a relevant image available, it can attach that image without asking to capture your screen.
