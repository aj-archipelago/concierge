## Managing Your Apps

Concierge supports various integrated apps and services that extend its functionality.

### Viewing Your Apps

- Click **Manage Apps** at the bottom of the sidebar
- Or navigate to the Apps section
- You'll see all available and connected applications

### Connecting Apps

- Browse the list of available integrations
- Click on an app to see its description and requirements
- Follow the setup instructions to connect it
- Connected apps appear in your sidebar navigation

### App Features

Connected apps may add:

- New sidebar navigation items
- Additional AI capabilities
- Specialized workflows and tools

### Tips

- Apps you connect are specific to your account
- Disconnect apps you no longer need from the Apps page
- Some apps may require additional permissions or API keys
